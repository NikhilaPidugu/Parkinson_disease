Parkinson’s Disease Prediction System

Parkinson’s disease is a progressive neurological disorder that affects movement, speech, and coordination. Early detection plays a crucial role in managing symptoms and improving the quality of life of patients.

This project uses machine learning to analyze voice-based biomedical data and provide an early indication of Parkinson’s disease in a non-invasive and efficient way.

About This Project

🧠 Problem

Parkinson’s disease is difficult to diagnose in early stages using only clinical observation. Traditional methods can be time-consuming and subjective.

🤖 Solution

We use machine learning models trained on voice features to automatically predict the likelihood of Parkinson’s disease from speech data.

📊 Features

Upload dataset, get predictions, see risk level, view explainable AI (SHAP) features, and download detailed reports.

⚠️ Disclaimer

This system is for academic and research purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis.
